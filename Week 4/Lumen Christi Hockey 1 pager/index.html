Lumen Christi Varsity Hockey Team

Team Website

A website that will showcase and inform visitors of updates and information regarding the Lumen Christi High School Hockey Team.

This website will be user friendly so players, families and fans can access information regarding the team. The website will be used to showcase team information such as the roster, schedule, scores, pictures and team announcements. The Lumen Christi Hockey team will use this website as a form of communication between coaches and players, alumni, and parents.
